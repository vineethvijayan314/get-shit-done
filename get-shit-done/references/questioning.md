# GSD Questioning

Protocol for `AskUserQuestion`.

## Rules
- **No small questions**: Research first. Evidence-based guesses first.
- **Opinionated**: "I assume X because [codebase evidence]. Correct? [Y/N]"
- **Atomic**: 1 query per turn.
- **Critical only**: Use only for design blockers or missing data.

## Types
- `intent`: Confirmation of goal.
- `choice`: Resolving A vs B.
- `blocker`: Cannot proceed without [input].
- `gate`: Approval of artifact.
